Licensed forensic psychologist (Cyprus)

Biography

Dimitra Papadopoulou was born in Athens, Greece. She read Psychology at DEREE - The American College of Greece and was awarded a BA Honours in Psychology in 2021. She then completed her Master's Degree in Forensic Psychology (MSc) at UCLAN University of Central Lancashire in Larnaca, Cyprus in 2024. She is a Licensed and Registered Forensic Psychologist in Cyprus and a Certified Family Mediator.

She joined Christos Th. Theodoulou LLC in October 2025 and currently works as an Office Administrator. As part of her role, Dimitra provides crucial support to the legal team by assisting with document drafting, case preparation and organization, client communication, court file management, and legal research where this is possible in both civil and criminal matters. Her efforts also focus on the psychological aspect of the case profile due to her expertise, and she contributes effectively to the smooth operation of the office and the delivery of high quality legal services.

Professional Summary

Experienced clinician working across forensic and clinical settings. Specialises in assessment, report-writing for legal contexts, expert witness testimony and CBT-based therapeutic interventions. Experience with substance use populations.

Services & Expertise

  • Forensic psychological assessments
  • Report writing for legal purposes
  • Expert witness testimony and consultancy
  • Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)
  • Treatment planning for substance use disorders
