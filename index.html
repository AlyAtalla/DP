About

Welcome to DP Forensic Psychology. I am Dimitra, a licensed and registered Forensic Psychologist practicing in Cyprus and Greece (Reg: 1033 / Reg: 3166). I remain dedicated to the intersection of mental health and the legal system and I provide high-level psychological services tailored to legal professionals, patients, and families. My practice is rooted in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), applied across both clinical and forensic populations. I bring extensive experience working with individuals navigating the complexities of addiction, while my approach is evidence-based, objective, and compassionate. Beyond therapeutic counseling, I specialize in forensic assessments, evaluations, and report writing and I offer expert witness testimony and consultancy for legal professionals. Whether you prefer face-to-face sessions or the convenience of online therapy, I am here to support you.
