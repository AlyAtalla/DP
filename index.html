About

Welcome to DP Forensic Psychology. I am Dimitra, a licensed and registered Forensic Psychologist practicing in Cyprus and Greece (Reg: 1033 / Reg: 3166). I remain dedicated to the intersection of mental health and the legal system and I provide high-level psychological services tailored to legal professionals, patients, and families. My practice is rooted in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), applied across both clinical and forensic populations. I bring extensive experience working with individuals navigating the complexities of addiction, while my approach is evidence-based, objective, and compassionate. Beyond therapeutic counseling, I specialize in forensic assessments, evaluations, and report writing and I offer expert witness testimony and consultancy for legal professionals. Whether you prefer face-to-face sessions or the convenience of online therapy, I am here to support you.

Cancellation Policy

Consistency is a key part of the therapeutic process. When we schedule a session, that time is reserved exclusively for you. If you need to cancel or reschedule, please provide at least 24 hours' notice.

  • Late Cancellation/No-Show Fee: If an appointment is missed or cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice, a fee of half the session amount will be applied. This fee must be settled prior to or at the time of our next scheduled session.
  • Emergency Exceptions: I understand that emergencies and sudden illnesses happen. Because of this, I offer one "grace" cancellation per year, where the late fee will be waived for a genuine emergency. Subsequent late cancellations will be charged regardless of the reason.
  • The Monday Rule: To avoid a late fee for Monday appointments, please notify me by the same time on the preceding Friday. This allows me the opportunity to offer that time to other clients before the weekend.
