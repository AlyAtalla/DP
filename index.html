DP Forensic Psychology

Dimitra Papadopoulou is a licensed and registered forensic psychologist (Cyprus). In-person and online therapy, assessments, forensic reports, expert witness testimony and consultancy. CBT-focused approach with experience in clinical and forensic populations and substance addiction.

About

I provide evidence-based psychological assessment and therapy, with a focus on Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT). I work with individuals across clinical and forensic settings, and have experience supporting people with substance use issues. I also prepare forensic reports for legal cases and provide expert testimony and consultancy to lawyers and courts.

Services

  • Individual CBT therapy (in-person & online)
  • Psychological assessments and evaluations
  • Forensic report writing for legal proceedings
  • Expert witness testimony and consultancy
  • Assessment & treatment planning for substance use
